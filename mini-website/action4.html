Small Changes, Big Impact

Conserving water and energy are powerful ways to fight climate change. Everyday actions such as turning off lights when not in use, fixing leaking faucets, and using energy-efficient appliances can make a big difference over time.

Water conservation is especially important as climate change increases the risk of drought. Reducing shower times, collecting rainwater for gardening, and choosing drought-resistant plants can help save water. Together, these efforts conserve resources and protect the environment.
