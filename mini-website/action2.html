The 3Rs: Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

Managing waste is a critical step in reducing greenhouse gas emissions. The 3Rs—Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle—help minimize the amount of waste that ends up in landfills and incinerators, which are major contributors to carbon emissions.

Reducing means buying only what we need and avoiding single-use plastics. Reusing involves finding new purposes for items instead of discarding them. Recycling ensures materials like paper, glass, and metal can be processed and made into new products, conserving resources and reducing energy use.
