Welcome to the Climate Change Explainer

Climate change is one of the most pressing challenges of our time. It affects ecosystems, weather patterns, sea levels, and the overall health of our planet. While it may seem overwhelming, there are concrete steps that individuals, communities, and nations can take to address it. This website serves as a simple guide to understand the causes of climate change and the actions we can all take to reduce its impact.
