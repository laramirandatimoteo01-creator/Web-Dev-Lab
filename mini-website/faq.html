Your Questions Answered

What is the biggest cause of climate change?
The burning of fossil fuels such as coal, oil, and gas is the largest contributor.
How can I help as an individual?
You can save energy, reduce waste, support renewable energy, and raise awareness.
Why is planting trees important?
Trees absorb carbon dioxide, clean the air, and provide habitats for wildlife.
